401(k) Plan Management IN 6 STEPS

6 easy steps to make your 401(k) plan management easy and scalable

  • 1. Initial Setup
    Enter basic information about your clients and their 401(k) plans
    We give you an easy to follow step by step guide so that can easily enter your necessary client information 401(k) plans making sure that you have all the information you need to get started. Setup takes under an hour.
  • 2. Data Entry
    Use our proprietary data tracking tool to easily takes of the actions you take for your 401(k) plans and the requirements that the given stakeholders need to complete so that your plans stay compliant.
  • 3 - Automatically Generate Reports
    Generate your 401(k) quarterly and annual reports in seconds not weeks
    Our system will take all of the notes that you have taken over the course of the quarter or year to automatically generate professional and personalized reports for each of your clients. All of your client reports are geneated in under 15 seconds.
  • 4 - Quickly Review
    Quickly check over each report generated for your clients
    Quickly review each report to make sure that it is accurate and that it meets your standards. If you see something that you do not like, you can easily edit the report to make sure that it is perfect.
  • 5 - Send to Clients
    Automatically send each report to clients
    Once you have reviewed each report you can automatically send them to all of your clients saving you the time of having to write out each email seperately.
  • 6 - Relax
    Relax, you are done
    Now just sit back, relax, and smile :)
