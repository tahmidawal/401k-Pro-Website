Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is 401(k) Pro?

401(k) Pro is a comprehensive platform that combines an app and spreadsheet to help 401(k) advisors manage their plans efficiently. It centralizes client information, automates reporting, provides regulatory guidance, and offers tools for comparing plans and managing requirements.

2. How does 401(k) Pro simplify 401(k) plan management?

401(k) Pro streamlines the process by centralizing all client information, automating quarterly and annual reporting, and providing easy access to regulatory information through built-in chatbots. This reduces the need to switch between multiple resources and ensures all data is up-to-date and accurate.

3. What types of reports can 401(k) Pro generate?

401(k) Pro automates the creation of quarterly and annual reports, ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements. These reports are comprehensive, accurate, and tailored to meet the needs of both advisors and their clients.

4. How does 401(k) Pro help with regulatory compliance?

401(k) Pro includes regulatory chatbots that provide guidance on ERISA, DOL, FINRA, and other relevant regulations. These chatbots help advisors understand and comply with the latest rules and requirements, reducing the risk of non-compliance.

5. Can 401(k) Pro help compare different 401(k) plans?

Yes, 401(k) Pro offers tools to easily compare various 401(k) plans, making it simpler to evaluate and select the best options for new clients or to review existing plans.

6. Is my data secure with 401(k) Pro?

Absolutely. 401(k) Pro employs advanced security measures, including encryption and secure data storage, to ensure that all client and plan information is protected and compliant with industry standards.
