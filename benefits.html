Frequently Asked Questions

What makes AccessibleAI different from traditional AI consultants?

We utilize a cost structure that is specifically designed to be accessible to small businesses. By allowing businesses to pay slowly over time rather than taking on massive up front fees, we are able to make AI accessible to businesses that would not be able to afford it otherwise.

What happens if I don't like the AI system you built for my business>

If you do not like the AI system that we built for you we will return your initial downpayment and you will not pay a penny for the work that we did for you. We are so confident in our ability to deliver that we are willing to put our money where our mouth is.

Chat GPT couldn't do what I needed for my business what makes you so sure that one of your systems can?

Generalized AI systems such as Chat GPT are designed to be decently good at a large range of tasks, rather than being able to do you business' unique need perfectly. This is why we purpose build an AI system for you that is designed to do exactly what you need it to do.

I don't know anything about AI or automation, is that ok?

Absolutely! You are no different from the vast majority of our clients. We are here to guide you through the entire process of finding and implementing AI and automation where it can help your business. Our mission is to make AI Accessible to everyone, not just AI experts!

What happens if I use my AI system for a month and then decide I do not want or need it anymore?

You can stop paying at any time! We do not require you to pay for the entire year if you decide that you do not want the system anymore. We are confident that you will love your system, but if you don't you can stop paying at any time. Our goal is to make AI accessible to everyone, and we do that by allowing small businesses to take on the least amount of risk possible when trying to adopt it!

Do I own the rights to the AI system you build for me?

It depends. When we build you a system, we own the rights to that system and essentially rent it to you for a low monthly fee. If you decide to stop paying for the system, we will take it back and you will no longer be able to use it. However, if you decide that you want or need to own the rights to the AI system we built for you, we are happy to sell it to you for an agreed upon price. In the end, it is a case of whatever works best for your business!

Do you provide periodic maintenance and testing of my AI system to make sure that it is performing well?

Yes! We provide periodic maintenance and testing of your AI system to make sure that it is performing well. Our goal is to keep you as our clients for as long as possible and an AI system that is not performing well is not worth much to you. We are here to make sure that your AI system is always performing at its best.
