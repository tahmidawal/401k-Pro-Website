Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is 401(k) Pro?

401(k) Pro is a plan management and report automation software to help 401(k) advisors manage their plans efficiently. It centralizes client information, automates management & reporting, provides regulatory guidance, and offers tools for comparing plans and managing requirements.

2. How does 401(k) Pro simplify 401(k) plan management?

401(k) Pro streamlines the process by centralizing all client information, streamlining data entry, automating quarterly and annual reporting, and providing easy access to regulatory information through built-in chatbots. This reduces the need to switch between multiple resources and ensures all data is up-to-date and accurate.

3. What types of reports can 401(k) Pro generate?

401(k) Pro automates the quarterly and annual reports, ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements. These reports are comprehensive, accurate, and tailored to meet the needs of both advisors and their clients.

4. How does 401k Pro help me manage my clients?

401k Pro streamlines client management with several powerful features:

Automatic Data Entry: During client meetings, you can jot down quick notes or "chicken scratch" on your plan touchpoints and checklist. Our AI rewriter then transforms these rough notes into polished, professional language, saving you time and ensuring accuracy.

Email Integration: If you receive an email from a client, simply forward it to auto-data-manager@401k-pro.ai. Our AI will automatically update the relevant plan touchpoints or other necessary information in the system.

AI Data Extraction: Upload your plan health reports or Form 5500, and our AI data extractor will effortlessly pull the necessary details, updating your plan performance or design information accordingly.
