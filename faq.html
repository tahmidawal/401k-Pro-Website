Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is 401(k) Pro?

401(k) Pro is a plan management and report automation software to help 401(k) advisors manage their plans efficiently. It centralizes client information, automates management & reporting, provides regulatory guidance, and offers tools for comparing plans and managing requirements.

2. How does 401(k) Pro simplify 401(k) plan management?

401(k) Pro streamlines the process by centralizing all client information, streamlining data entry, automating quarterly and annual reporting, and providing easy access to regulatory information through built-in chatbots. This reduces the need to switch between multiple resources and ensures all data is up-to-date and accurate.

3. What types of reports can 401(k) Pro generate?

401(k) Pro automates the quarterly and annual reports, ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements. These reports are comprehensive, accurate, and tailored to meet the needs of both advisors and their clients.

4. How does 401k Pro help me manage my clients?

401k Pro streamlines client management with several powerful features:

Automatic Data Entry: During client meetings, you can jot down quick notes or "chicken scratch" on your plan touchpoints and checklist. Our AI rewriter then transforms these rough notes into polished, professional language, saving you time and ensuring accuracy.

Email Integration: If you receive an email from a client, simply forward it to auto-data-manager@401k-pro.ai. Our AI will automatically update the relevant plan touchpoints or other necessary information in the system.

AI Data Extraction: Upload your plan health reports or Form 5500, and our AI data extractor will effortlessly pull the necessary details, updating your plan performance or design information accordingly.

5. Can 401(k) Pro help compare different 401(k) plans?

Yes, 401(k) Pro offers tools to easily compare various 401(k) plans, making it simpler to evaluate and select the best options for new clients or to review existing plans.

6. Is my data secure with 401(k) Pro?

Absolutely. 401(k) Pro employs advanced security measures, including encryption at rest and at transtion, to ensure that all client and plan information is protected and compliant with industry standards.

7. Should I trust AI with my data?

Yes, you can trust AI with your data. At 401(k) Pro, we house our AI models internally, ensuring that there is no chance of tech platforms training off of your data. Additionally, if you prefer, you have the option to turn off all AI features.

8. How easy is it to get started with 401(k) Pro?

Getting started with 401(k) Pro is simple. Sign up for an account, input your client data, and you can begin managing your 401(k) plans right away. Our user-friendly interface and comprehensive guides make the setup process quick and straightforward. This process should take no longer than an hour.

9. What kind of support does 401(k) Pro offer?

401(k) Pro offers extensive support, including online tutorials, user guides, and a dedicated customer support team available via email and phone to assist with any questions or issues you may encounter.

10. Can I try 401(k) Pro before committing?

Yes, we offer a free trial period so you can explore the features and benefits of 401(k) Pro before making a commitment. Sign up on our website to start your free trial today.

401(k) Pro includes Regulatory Chatbots that provide guidance on ERISA, DOL, FINRA, and other relevant regulations. These chatbots help answers advisors questions with detailed citations and referecence allowing them to understand and comply with the latest rules and requirements, reducing the risk of non-compliance.
